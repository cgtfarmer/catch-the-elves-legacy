Catch the Elves


Instructions:

You are the red square, and the goal is to "catch" all the elves as quickly as possible

Controls

  • Difficulty: Use the difficulty buttons to select the desired difficulty

  • Motion: Character's movement may be controlled with the arrow keys

  • Catching: The catching action may be performed by pressing the spacebar. The action will only be successful if an elf is within the "square outline" surrounding the player
